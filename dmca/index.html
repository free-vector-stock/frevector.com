How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

To be valid, your DMCA takedown notice must include all of the following:

  1. Your electronic or physical signature as the copyright owner or authorized representative.
  2. A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  3. The full URL of the content on frevector.com that you believe infringes your copyright.
  4. Your full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  5. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  6. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
