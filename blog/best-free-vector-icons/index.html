Best Free Vector Icons for Web Design Projects

Icons are the visual language of the web. They help users navigate, understand content, and add a professional touch to any interface. Here are the best free vector icon sets for your next project.

1. Font Awesome (Free Version)

Font Awesome is the most popular icon toolkit. Their free version includes hundreds of essential icons that are easy to use via CSS or as direct SVG downloads.

2. Google Material Icons

Designed by Google, these icons follow the Material Design guidelines. They are clean, consistent, and perfect for Android apps or modern web interfaces.

3. Lucide Icons

Lucide is a beautiful, open-source icon library that is a community-run fork of Feather Icons. They are lightweight, stroke-based, and very customizable.

4. frevector.com Icon Category

At frevector.com, we have a dedicated category for icons. Whether you need social media logos, UI elements, or specialized business icons, you can find them in SVG and JPEG formats for free.

Why Use Vector Icons?

Vector icons (SVG) are superior to icon fonts because they are more accessible, easier to style, and don't suffer from "flicker" issues during page load. They are also much easier to animate and manipulate with modern web technologies.
